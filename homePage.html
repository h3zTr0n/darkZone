Ehita Vision Plan

This plan should focus on opportunity, which will also inspire all computer students to want to become members. This should also inspire collaboration and team work among computer students towards their shared gaols. The term opportunity in this plan is further divided into three parts, namely

  • Exposure
  • Experience
  • Educational advancement

This plan also seeks to upgrade the learning situation and capacity of computer students, this could be done through installing projectors, projector screens, sound system and window blinders, in computer students lecture rooms.

Experience in this plan is defined as the knowledge or skills gained over time due to training or exposure to related computer work, or any relevant training complementing the major programme. All EHITA members should feel confident enough with their abilities and qualifications, through the experience they are entitled to attain while on campus. This would also help them realise their worth in the industry and not settle for less, than that which they deserve.
